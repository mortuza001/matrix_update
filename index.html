Infomatrix Firmware Update Guide

Please follow these steps to update your device firmware.

Prerequisites:

  • A PC or Laptop with a USB port.
  • A standard USB data cable. Usually MicroUSB.
  • Appropriate screwdriver.

Step 1: Access the Hardware

  1. Locate the side of the device cover that displays the "Infomatrix" label.
  2. Using a screwdriver, carefully remove the screws securing that side of cover.
  3. Remove the cover to expose the internal components.
  4. Locate the microcontroller's USB port inside the device.
  5. Connect the device to your PC using the USB cable.

Step 2: Prepare to Flash - Firmware upgade

  1. Open a web browser on your PC (Google Chrome or Edge).
  2. Select your Infomatrix device model and desired version from the dropdown menu.


  1. Click the "Flash" button.
  2. A system pop-up window will appear asking you to select a connection port.
  3. Identify the correct COM Port associated with your connected device.
  4. Select the port and click "Connect" and "Install" to begin the process.
